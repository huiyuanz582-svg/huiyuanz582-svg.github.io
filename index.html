01 · About

Recovering reliable 3D structure from noisy real-world data.

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. at Beijing Institute of Technology. Before that, I received my master's degree in Control Engineering from Northeastern University. My work explores geometry-aware and learning-based solutions for point cloud restoration, with particular interest in challenging scenes affected by dust and measurement noise.
